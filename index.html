Intersting Facts About Teddy Bear

  • Teddy bears got their name from the story that Teddy Roosevelt refused to shoot a bear cub while on a hunting trip in 1902.
  • The Oxford English Dictionary dates the first use of the term teddy bear to 1906.
  • The world's largest stitched teddy bear is proudly American! The 55-foot-4 creation was made in 2008
